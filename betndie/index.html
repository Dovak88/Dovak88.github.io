BET’N’DIE

Online PC Game • 4 players FFA • UE4 • Wwise

BET’n’DIE is an online FFA FPS where the most daring gamblers compete in a space arena by piloting ARCs robots.

This is my graduation project, made by working with 19 people. For the audio, I worked with Baptiste Denoux as a composer and sound designer.

I was in charge of the majority of weapons SFX, announcer voice design, machines SFX, foleys, as well as some music production. But I was mainly responsible of all the technical sound design and blueprint integration. I worked on a lot of interactive system using blueprints for handling the announcer integration, all the sound design playback or the interactive music.

On top of that, I was in charge of the audio spatialization, from the audio level design (AkGometry / AkPortal / AkRoom) to the fine tuning of all the Attenuation Sharesets.
In the end, I worked closely with the developers on debuging and optimizing the game, audio-wise.

→ This is my most advanced work so far, so don’t forget to check out the related documents for more information.
